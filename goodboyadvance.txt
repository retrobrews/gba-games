GOODBOY ADVANCE
by: exelotl (Jeremy Clarke)
on: 2018-12-04

A short exploration platformer for the Gameboy Advance, about a dog and his red rocket.
A Ludum Dare 43 Jam game by @exelotl and @hot_pengu, for the theme:
'Sacrifices Must Be Made'.

This games gfx/music/sfx & programming, was fully developed in just 3 days!
There is also a playable browser version of the game which you can find here --> https://hotpengu.itch.io/goodboyadvance
Please give your support (https://hotpengu.itch.io/goodboyadvance) if you enjoy it!
