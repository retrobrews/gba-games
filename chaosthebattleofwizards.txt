Chaos – The Battle Of Wizards V0.82
BY QUIRKY

Chaos is a tactical combat video game based on the 1985 Spectrum game ‘Chaos – The Battle of Wizards’. 
Up to 8 wizards compete in a turn-based battle to the death, involving elves, dragons, fire, lightning and the odd Gooey Blob. 
The last wizard standing is the winner.
