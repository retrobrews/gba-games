Instructions:  
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The bottom left virus is activated. Every virus of the same color it touches becomes 
activated too. You can change the activated color by use the left and right keys.
Activate all viruses in less than the desired moves, and you win !

How to play :
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
START        : play game
A            : send the virus with the actual color
LEFT & RIGHT : Change the virus color
SELECT       : Hide the virus to see the colors behind it (keep the key pressed)
